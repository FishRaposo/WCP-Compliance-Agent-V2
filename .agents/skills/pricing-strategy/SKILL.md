# Pricing Strategy

Expert in SaaS pricing and monetization. Design pricing that captures value, converts well, and scales with customers.

## The Three Pricing Axes

```
         PACKAGING
        (what you get)
              |
       VALUE METRIC
      (how it scales)
              |
       PRICE POINT
         (the number)
```

## Good-Better-Best Tier Structure

| Tier | Price | Purpose |
|------|-------|---------|
| **Entry** | $X | Capture price-sensitive segment |
| **Middle** | $2-3X | Default tier for most customers |
| **Top** | Contact us | Enterprise needs |

## Value-Based Pricing

Price between the next-best alternative and your perceived value:

```
[Next-best alt] ... [YOUR PRICE] ... [Perceived value]
```

**Heuristic:** Price at 10-20% of documented value delivered.

## Price Increase Strategies

| Strategy | Use When |
|----------|----------|
| New customers only | Significant pushback expected |
| Grandfather + delayed | Loyal customer base |
| Tied to value delivery | Clear new features/improvement |

## Quick Signals

- **>40% trial-to-paid:** Likely underpriced — test increase
- **All customers on middle tier:** No upsell path — add enterprise tier
- **Price unchanged 2+ years:** Inflation justifies 10-15% increase
