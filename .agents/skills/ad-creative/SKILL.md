# Ad Creative

Expert performance creative strategist. Generate high-performing ad creative at scale.

## Platform Specs

### Google Ads (Responsive Search Ads)

| Element | Limit | Quantity |
|---------|-------|----------|
| Headline | 30 characters | Up to 15 |
| Description | 90 characters | Up to 4 |

**RSA rules:**
- Headlines must make sense independently and in any combination
- Include at least one keyword-focused headline
- Include at least one benefit-focused headline
- Include at least one CTA headline

### Meta Ads (Facebook/Instagram)

| Element | Limit | Notes |
|---------|-------|-------|
| Primary text | 125 chars visible (up to 2,200) | Front-load the hook |
| Headline | 40 characters | Below the image |
| Description | 30 characters | Below headline |

### LinkedIn Ads

| Element | Limit | Notes |
|---------|-------|-------|
| Intro text | 150 chars recommended | Above the image |
| Headline | 70 chars recommended | Below the image |
| Description | 100 chars recommended | Appears in some placements |

## Angles to Test

| Category | Example |
|----------|---------|
| Pain point | "Stop wasting time on X" |
| Outcome | "Achieve Y in Z days" |
| Social proof | "Join 10,000+ teams who..." |
| Curiosity | "The X secret top companies use" |
| Urgency | "Limited time: get X free" |

## Headlines That Click

- Specific: "Cut reporting time 75%"
- Benefits: "Ship code faster"
- Active voice: "Automate your reports"
- Include numbers: "3x faster," "in 5 minutes"

## Common Mistakes

- Headlines that only work together (RSA randomizes)
- Ignoring character limits
- No CTA headlines
- Retiring creative too early (need 1,000+ impressions)
