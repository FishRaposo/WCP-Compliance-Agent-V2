# Content Research Writer

Writing partner for researching, outlining, drafting, and refining content while maintaining your voice.

## When to Use

- Blog posts, articles, newsletters
- Educational content or tutorials
- Thought leadership pieces
- Case studies with sources
- Technical documentation with citations

## Workflow

### 1. Collaborative Outlining
Structure ideas into coherent outlines with research gaps identified.

### 2. Research Assistance
Find relevant information and add proper citations.

### 3. Hook Improvement
Strengthen openings to capture attention.

**Hook Types:**
- Data-driven: "Last month, I asked AI to analyze 500 interviews..."
- Question: "What if you could talk to every customer...?"
- Story: "Sarah spent two weeks building the wrong feature..."

### 4. Section Feedback
Review each section as you write for:
- Clarity
- Flow
- Evidence
- Style consistency

### 5. Voice Preservation
- Learn from your existing writing samples
- Suggest, don't replace
- Enhance, don't override

## Citation Formats

**Inline:**
> Studies show 40% improvement (McKinsey, 2024).

**Numbered:**
> Studies show 40% productivity improvement [1].
> [1] McKinsey Global Institute. (2024)...

## File Organization

```
your-project/
├── outline.md
├── research.md
├── draft-v1.md
├── draft-v2.md
└── final.md
```

## Best Practices

- Work in sections, get feedback incrementally
- Version your drafts (v1, v2, final)
- Save research separately
- Read aloud to catch clunky sentences
