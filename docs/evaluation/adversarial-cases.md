## Core adversarial categories

- OCR failures and low-quality scans
- partial or missing signatures
- contradictory totals across daily and weekly records
- job title synonym drift
- locality mismatches
- stale corpus versions
- retrieval conflicts where multiple DBWD sections look plausible
- deterministic/model disagreement
- malformed uploads and missing attachments

## Why these matter

These are the kinds of failures that expose whether the system is truly production-minded or only built for happy-path demos.
