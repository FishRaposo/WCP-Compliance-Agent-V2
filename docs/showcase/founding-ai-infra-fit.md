## What this repo signals

This repository signals strength in the areas a founding AI infrastructure role usually cares about:

- deterministic scaffolding instead of model-only behavior,
- retrieval and context as infrastructure,
- schema-bound outputs and typed failure modes,
- evaluation as a deploy gate,
- platform thinking across APIs, data, and operations.

## Public translation of the stack

- current implementation is TypeScript-first,
- target architecture is documented in the language of real AI infra systems:
  - warehouse-backed truth,
  - transcript/search retrieval,
  - cached operational context,
  - hybrid search and reranking,
  - CI-backed evaluations,
  - traceable operations and cost controls.

## Honest positioning

The strongest public framing is:
